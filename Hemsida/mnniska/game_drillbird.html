Drillbird

March 2025

Platforms: Web Browser, PC & Mac
Team: Developed by myself with contributions from friends with art and music.
Genre: Digging/Platforming Adventure
Rresponsibilities: Creative vision, Game Design, Programming, Art
Itch.io page

Drillbird is a ~1-5h digging platformer where you play as a bird with a drill as its beak, drilling your way through the world to find ores in order to make your egg hatch.

I developed the game over three months after the studio I previously worked at shut down. It's a good testament to my game design, planning and programming skills.

Responsibilities

  • Designed the game, from all of the rules driving the game design, to the hand crafted world.
  • Coded the entire game using Godots GDScript.
  • Planned the projects scope to ensure it could be delivered within the narrow 4 month window.
  • Drew some of the art - including the characters, UI, and a first pass for environments and cutscenes.
  • Headed the project, providing guidance and hype for Drillbirds other contributors
